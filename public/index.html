About

Paint Editor is a lightweight digital art application built for artists, designers, and hobbyists.

Our goal is to make digital painting accessible for everyone — fast, simple, and free of unnecessary complexity.

Whether you’re sketching ideas or finishing detailed artwork, Paint Editor provides intuitive and smooth performance on any Windows device. We designed it to be fast, minimal, and inspiring.
